二、Court Opinion Elements（法庭意见书要素）

1. Nature of Case（案件性质）

A landmark constitutional civil rights case heard by the U.S. Supreme Court. The dispute focuses on whether a Louisiana state law mandating railroad companies to provide "separate but equal" passenger accommodations for white and colored races violates the 13th and 14th Amendments to the U.S. Constitution. This case defined the legal basis for racial segregation in the U.S. for nearly 60 years.

点击显示中文

美国最高法院审理的标志性宪法民权案件。争议焦点为路易斯安那州一项要求铁路公司为白人和有色人种提供"隔离但平等"乘客设施的法律，是否违反美国宪法第13和第14修正案。该案为美国近60年的种族隔离制度奠定了法律基础。

2. Concise Rule of Law（核心法律规则）

  1. The 13th Amendment (abolishing slavery) only prohibits slavery and involuntary servitude; it does not ban "reasonable racial segregation" that merely creates legal distinctions between races without imposing conditions of bondage.

    点击显示中文

    第13修正案（废除奴隶制）仅禁止奴隶制和非自愿劳役，不禁止"合理的种族隔离"——即仅在种族间创设法律区分、未施加奴役条件的制度。

  2. The 14th Amendment (guaranteeing equal protection of the laws and due process) applies only to legal and political equality, not social equality. States may exercise their police power to enact laws requiring racial segregation in public spaces (e.g., public transportation) as long as the segregated facilities are "substantially equal" in quality.

    点击显示中文

    第14修正案（保障法律平等保护与正当程序）仅适用于法律和政治平等，不适用于社会平等。各州可行使治安权，制定要求公共空间（如公共交通）种族隔离的法律，只要隔离设施在质量上"实质平等"。

  3. States have the authority to independently define racial classification standards (e.g., blood proportion thresholds for identifying "colored persons"), and such definitions fall within state legislative jurisdiction unless they directly violate federal constitutional principles.

    点击显示中文

    各州有权自主定义种族分类标准（如认定"有色人种"的血统比例阈值），此类定义属于州立法管辖权范围，除非直接违反联邦宪法原则。

3. Facts（案件事实）

  • Homer Plessy, a U.S. citizen and Louisiana resident, had mixed ancestry: seven-eighths Caucasian and one-eighth African. His African heritage was not visually discernible, and he was legally entitled to the same rights as white citizens.

    点击显示中文

    荷马·普莱西（美国公民、路易斯安那州居民）为混血血统：八分之七白人血统、八分之一非洲血统。其非洲血统从外貌无法辨别，且依法享有与白人公民同等的权利。

  • On June 7, 1892, Plessy purchased a first-class ticket for a train operated by the East Louisiana Railway (traveling from New Orleans to Covington, both within Louisiana) and occupied a seat in the coach designated for white passengers.

    点击显示中文

    1892年6月7日，普莱西购买了东路易斯安那铁路公司（往返于路易斯安那州新奥尔良与科文顿之间）的一等车票，并坐在指定给白人乘客的车厢座位上。

  • The East Louisiana Railway, acting under a Louisiana state law passed in 1890 (requiring separate accommodations for white and colored races), ordered Plessy to move to the coach reserved for colored passengers. Plessy refused.

    点击显示中文

    东路易斯安那铁路公司依据1890年路易斯安那州通过的法律（要求为白人和有色人种提供隔离设施），命令普莱西迁往有色人种专用车厢，普莱西拒绝服从。

  • Plessy was forcibly removed from the white passengers' coach by police and imprisoned in the New Orleans parish jail. He was charged with violating the 1890 Louisiana segregation law.

    点击显示中文

    警方将普莱西从白人车厢强行带离，并关押在新奥尔良教区监狱。普莱西被指控违反1890年路易斯安那州隔离法。

  • Plessy challenged the constitutionality of the Louisiana law, arguing it violated the 13th and 14th Amendments. The Louisiana Supreme Court rejected his claim, prompting Plessy to appeal to the U.S. Supreme Court.

    点击显示中文

    普莱西质疑该州法的合宪性，主张其违反第13和第14修正案。路易斯安那州最高法院驳回其诉求，普莱西遂上诉至美国最高法院。

  • The 1890 Louisiana law required railroad companies to provide either separate coaches or partitioned sections for white and colored passengers; violations (e.g., a passenger occupying a coach of the other race) carried penalties of a $25 fine or up to 20 days in jail.

    点击显示中文

    1890年路易斯安那州法要求铁路公司为白人和有色人种提供独立车厢或分隔区域；违反者（如乘客占用另一种族的车厢）将面临25美元罚款或最长20天监禁。

4. Issue（争议焦点）

Does the Louisiana state law mandating railroad companies to provide "separate but equal" passenger accommodations for white and colored races violate the U.S. Constitution's 13th Amendment (prohibiting slavery/involuntary servitude) and 14th Amendment (guaranteeing equal protection and due process)?

点击显示中文

路易斯安那州要求铁路公司为白人和有色人种提供"隔离但平等"乘客设施的法律，是否违反美国宪法第13修正案（禁止奴隶制/非自愿劳役）和第14修正案（保障平等保护与正当程序）？

5. Holding（判决理由）

  1. 13th Amendment Challenge

    The Louisiana law does not violate the 13th Amendment. Racial segregation merely creates a legal distinction based on skin color, not a condition of slavery or involuntary servitude. The 13th Amendment's purpose is to eliminate bondage, not to regulate social interactions or public space usage between races.

    点击显示中文

    第13修正案相关争议：该州法未违反第13修正案。种族隔离仅基于肤色创设法律区分，并非奴隶制或非自愿劳役状态。第13修正案的目的是消除奴役，而非规制种族间的社会交往或公共空间使用。

  2. 14th Amendment Challenge

    • The 14th Amendment's "Equal Protection Clause" does not require "social equality" (e.g., racial integration in public transportation); it only mandates that states not deny legal rights based on race. As long as segregated facilities (e.g., railroad coaches) are equal in quality, the "equal protection" requirement is satisfied.

      点击显示中文

      第14修正案"平等保护条款"不要求"社会平等"（如公共交通中的种族融合），仅要求各州不得基于种族剥夺法律权利。只要隔离设施（如铁路车厢）质量平等，即满足"平等保护"要求。

    • States have the authority to exercise police power to regulate public order and comfort. Segregation laws are a reasonable exercise of this power, as they align with societal customs and traditions and help maintain public peace.

      点击显示中文

      各州有权行使治安权以规制公共秩序与舒适度。隔离法是该权力的合理行使，因其符合社会习俗与传统，且有助于维护公共和平。

  3. Racial Classification

    The question of whether Plessy was legally "colored" under Louisiana law is irrelevant to the case's core issue (the constitutionality of the segregation law itself). States may define racial categories independently, and such definitions do not inherently violate federal law.

    点击显示中文

    种族分类：根据路易斯安那州法，普莱西是否为法律意义上的"有色人种"，与案件核心争议（隔离法本身的合宪性）无关。各州可自主定义种族类别，此类定义本身不违反联邦法律。

6. Decision（判决结果）

Affirmed.（维持原判）

The U.S. Supreme Court affirmed the judgment of the Louisiana Supreme Court. The Louisiana law mandating "separate but equal" railroad accommodations was declared constitutional. Plessy's claim that the law violated the 13th and 14th Amendments was rejected. This decision established the "separate but equal" doctrine as the legal foundation for racial segregation in the U.S. until it was overturned in Brown v. Board of Education (1954). Justice John Marshall Harlan filed a dissenting opinion, arguing the law was inherently discriminatory and violated the Constitution's "color-blind" principle.

点击显示中文

美国最高法院维持路易斯安那州最高法院的判决，认定要求铁路"隔离但平等"设施的州法合宪，驳回普莱西关于该法违反第13和第14修正案的主张。该判决确立"隔离但平等"原则为美国种族隔离的法律基础，直至1954年《布朗诉教育委员会案》（Brown v. Board of Education）将其推翻。约翰·马歇尔·哈伦大法官发表异议意见，主张该法本质上具有歧视性，违反宪法的"无色盲"原则。
